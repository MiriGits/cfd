Events

Bake sale



Requirements for participation:

  • Acceptable bake sale foods include items like bars, brownies, cookies, cakes, breads, fruit pies, and doughnuts.
  • Not allowed: meat or cream-filled pastries, eclairs, and cream pies.
  • Canned, pre-made frostings can be used.
  • All bake sale items shall be individually wrapped at the original point of preparation. Items to remain wrapped until sold.
  • A sign or placard stating "homemade/not inspected" must be posted at the sale site.

Bag Tournament



How to play:

  • Ask a co-worker to form a team.
  • Fill out the signup form below.
  • Or, fill out a submission form in the lunchroom.
  • $10 per team. You will pay when you play.
  • There will be three sets of boards at PSB and three sets at UMS.
  • Winning team gets to choose which federation receives the money.
    • Winners also get to draw a prize and will receive screenprinted shirts.

Federations

Hover above each icon to learn about their mission. For further information and to donate to one of the federations below, visit the University's Community Fund Drive page.
Community Health Charities Help their member charities to provide crucial health research, prevention, and support services that directly impact those affected by chronic illness.
Community Shares Their mission is to create a fair, just, and equitable Minnesota. They fund 52 nonprofit members who use the arts and community action to fight social injustices.
Minnesota Environmental Fund Help protect, conserve, and restore Minnesota's environment by supporting more than 20 organizations.
United Way Connecting people and resources within our community to challenge and change systems that limit our potential. For example, $5 per pay period provides 25 nutritious meals for a family of four.

Open Your Heart Helping hungry and homeless Minnesotans since 1986. Open Your Heart helps food and shelter providers get the things they need to serve more people.
UMN Foundation Donate to help students follow their dreams, researchers to pursue new cures and discoveries, and outreach programs to transform our communities.
UNCF Administering more than 400 programs, including scholarship, internship and fellowship, mentoring, summer enrichment, and curriculum and faculty development programs.
